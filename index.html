Directorate of Tourism (DOT)

Directorate of Tourism, the flagship body of Maharashtra Tourism, looks after implementing and introducing various tourisms schemes, promotins and publicity to boost tourisms in the State.Since the inception of the Directorate Of Tourism, Govermment of Maharashtra, the State has attained several milestones and bagged numerous accomplishments with the help of various initialitives.

Mentioned below are some of the key highlights/achievements of the past one year


1.Introduction of single window clearance for licensing to promote ease of doing business for hospitality sector As an effect to step up and review tourism in the state, Maharashtra has introduced a single-window clearance for licensing in order to paramote ease of doing business for hospitality Sector. Leading the way to boost the tourism sector, in Maharashtra only 10,not 70, licence will be needed for hospitality business. Also, the decision of merging the application forms with self-declaration estiminated the requirement of a permission letter. This means reduction in paper work.
